Validity, universality and structure of the Ginzburg-Landau equation.

Equadiff 99, International Conference on Differential Equations , (B. Fiedler et al. eds.) World Scientific, Singapore, 2000, 186-200.

Ian Melbourne

Abstract

We present new results on the validity, universality and structure of the Ginzburg-Landau equation on the line.
