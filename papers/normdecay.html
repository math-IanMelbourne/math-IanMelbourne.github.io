Decay in norm of transfer operators for semiflows

Studia Math. 266 (2022) 149-166

Ian Melbourne, Nicolò Paviato and Dalia Terhesiu

Abstract We establish exponential decay in Hölder norm of transfer operators applied to smooth observables of uniformly and nonuniformly expanding semiflows with exponential decay of correlations.
