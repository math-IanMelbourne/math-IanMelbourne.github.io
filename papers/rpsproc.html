Bifurcation from periodic and relative periodic solutions in equivariant dynamical systems

Equadiff 99, International Conference on Differential Equations , (B. Fiedler et al. eds.) World Scientific, Singapore, 2000, 175-185.

Jeroen Lamb, Ian Melbourne and Claudia Wulff

Abstract

We review recent developments in the theory for generic bifurcation from periodic and relative periodic solutions in equivariant dynamical systems.
