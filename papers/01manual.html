On the Implementation of the 0-1 Test for Chaos.

SIAM J. Appl. Dyn. Sys. 8 (2009) 129-145

Georg Gottwald and Ian Melbourne

Abstract

In this paper we address practical aspects of the implementation of the 0-1 test for chaos. In addition, we present a new formulation of the test which significantly increases its sensitivity. The test can be viewed as a method to distill a binary quantity from the power spectrum. The implementation is guided by recent results from the theoretical justification of the test as well as by exploring better statistical methods to determine the binary quantities. We give several examples to illustrate the improvement.
