Comment on ``Reliability of the 0-1 test for chaos''

Phys. Rev. E 77 (2008) 028201.

Georg Gottwald and Ian Melbourne

Abstract

Recently Hu, Tung, Gao and Cao investigated the 0-1 test for chaos. By looking at random data and at data stemming from the one-dimensional logistic map they concluded that the test is unreliable. We explain why their criticism is unfounded.
