Good inducing schemes for uniformly hyperbolic maps

Ann. Henri Poincaré 26 (2025) 921-945.

Ian Melbourne and Paulo Varandas

Abstract

These notes are not intended for publication. However, it seemed useful to have the arguments in Section 6 of L.-S. Young, Statistical properties of dynamical systems with some hyperbolicity, Ann. of Math. 147 (1998) 585-650 written in a simplified form, in preparation for the case of flows. One definition is simplified, therefore bypassing an unclear argument.
